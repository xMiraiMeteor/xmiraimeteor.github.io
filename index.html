FAQ

Questions,
answered

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach our team at hello@clearwave.io — we reply within 2 hours.

Is there a free trial?
+
Yes — every plan starts with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You get full access to all features in your chosen tier so you can make a real evaluation before committing.
How does pricing work for larger teams?
+
Starter and Professional plans are per-workspace, not per-seat — so you won't see surprise bills as your team grows. Enterprise plans are custom-quoted based on your specific needs and contract length.
Can I migrate data from another tool?
+
We support CSV imports and direct migration from Notion, Airtable, Asana, and Trello. Enterprise customers get a dedicated migration specialist who handles the entire process for you.
What does the 99.9% uptime SLA mean?
+
It means Clearwave is contractually committed to less than 8.7 hours of downtime per year. We monitor availability 24/7, post all incidents publicly at status.clearwave.io, and issue credits automatically if SLA is breached.
Is my data secure?
+
Clearwave is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and ISO 27001 aligned. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You can request a full security report from our compliance team at any time.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. There are no lock-in contracts on monthly plans. Cancel from your account settings at any time and you won't be charged again. Annual plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled to stop renewal.
